ðŸ”—
This construct is not XSS safe, see: Section "xss-unsafe (--xss-unsafe)".

Here for example we define a paragraph in raw HTML: 
\passthrough[[
<p>Hello <b>raw</b> HTML!</p>
]]
which renders as:

Hello raw HTML!
